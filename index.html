About Us

Our Sweet Indian Heritage

Since 1995, Mithai Ice Cream has been serving authentic Indian ice cream flavors that capture the essence of traditional Indian sweets. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, perfected to delight modern palates.

We use fresh milk from local dairy farms, seasonal fruits, and natural ingredients to create ice cream that's rich in flavor and true to its Indian roots.
